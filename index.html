So we decided to come up with a cross-country platform that would help us inform, teach, and help youth (who are the decision-makers of tomorrow) get better knowledge to face the different challenges their communities or the world is going through.

    vision and mission

    Vision

    To enhance the upcoming generation of the world. Empower their personality in such a way they are more confident in life. We are looking forward to making them Global changemakers of this planet. We believe that despite being divided by Nations, we can make the world better by being United and facing together challenges.

    Mission

    This platform will provide the young generation of the world with the right skills, wisdom, and opportunities to nurture them towards a strong future. We want to create more givers towards humanity rather than takers. Our main purpose is to make them well prepared for the upcoming challenges of the world and know their role to play. We are trying to bring out the best in you.

    Growth and Success of youth minds are the main purpose of this organization.

    To get together the youth who have the burning desire to share a helping hand for the problems which are taking place in the world. To act as Global Citizens and contribute in changing the solving planet issues.

    • 01 What is this organization about?
      • A platform called International Circle of Youth ( I C Y ) for empowering youth from all around the world.
      • This platform will consist of a series of programs in different areas of expertise. The activities include Virtual Exchange Program, Summit, Training, Workshop, Virtual Camp, etc.
      • The areas of expertise focus on conflict resolution and peacebuilding, youth development, sustainable development, education, and gender equality.
      • We will collaborate with non-profit organizations (NGOs), government organizations, universities, and schools.
      • To develop holistic youths covering all possible aspects for sustainable development.
    • 02 Target audience

      Every youth (the nearest future decision-makers) with passion to make changes. We will focus on High School and University Students

    • 03 Launch Timeline

      The platform will be launched in summer 2022 starting with the first edition of ToLEx ( Tomorrow Leaders Exchange Program).

     

    Objectives

    ICY has set certain goals to achieve for the youth.

    • To provide youth with resources and knowledge to thrive in their life.

    • Become more self-aware and responsible.

    • Break stereotypes and Bridge differences between countries.

    • They will be enhancing their core competencies and coming closer to an interconnected world.

    • They will be contributing to developing their communities.

    • To make them more empathetic toward the world and their communities.
